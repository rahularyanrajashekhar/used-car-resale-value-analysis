# Used Car Resale Value Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a dataset of 1,997 used vehicle sales records to identify the key factors influencing resale value. The objective was to help a used-car dealership make data-driven decisions regarding vehicle acquisition, pricing, and inventory management.

The analysis focuses on understanding how vehicle characteristics such as age, mileage, fuel type, transmission type, vehicle dimensions, and fuel tank capacity affect selling prices. Statistical techniques including descriptive analysis, correlation analysis, and regression modelling were used to uncover actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem

Used car dealerships need to accurately evaluate vehicle prices to maximize profitability and reduce pricing risks. This project aims to identify the variables that have the strongest impact on resale value and provide recommendations to improve purchasing and sales strategies.

---

## Key Findings

### Vehicle Age is a Major Price Driver

Vehicle age showed a stronger negative relationship with selling price than mileage, indicating that depreciation significantly impacts resale value.

### Mileage Impacts Price

Higher kilometers driven generally resulted in lower resale values, although the relationship was weaker than vehicle age.

### Automatic Vehicles Generate Higher Value

Automatic transmission vehicles produced higher overall sales value compared to manual vehicles.

### Hybrid Vehicles Show Strong Potential

Hybrid vehicles achieved premium resale prices and may represent future growth opportunities.

### Larger Vehicles Command Higher Prices

Vehicle length and fuel tank capacity demonstrated positive correlations with selling price.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Prioritize acquisition of newer vehicles.
* Focus on automatic transmission vehicles.
* Increase inventory of hybrid vehicles where market demand exists.
* Consider vehicle dimensions and specifications during valuation.
* Develop automated pricing models to identify undervalued vehicles.
